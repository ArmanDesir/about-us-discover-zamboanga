Our Story

Built for Zamboanga, by People Who Care

DiscoverZamboanga was born out of a simple observation: Zamboanga City is home to thousands of hardworking local businesses, yet many of them struggle to be found online. Customers couldn't find them, and business owners had no easy, affordable way to establish a digital presence.

That gap is what we set out to close. We created a dedicated platform — built specifically for Zamboanga — where every local business, from a small neighborhood sari-sari store to a well-established restaurant, can be discovered by anyone searching for services in the city.

Today, DiscoverZamboanga stands as the city's most focused and community-driven business directory, committed to making local commerce more visible, accessible, and connected.

"Our goal is simple if you're looking for a business in Zamboanga, this is where you find it. And if you own a business here, this is where you belong."

— The DiscoverZamboanga Team
Why Zamboanga

A City Worth Discovering

Zamboanga City is one of the Philippines' most unique and culturally rich cities. Here's why we chose to build this platform here.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Known as "Asia's Latin City," Zamboanga boasts a unique blend of Filipino, Spanish, and indigenous cultures — a city unlike any other in the country.

Growing Local Economy

Zamboanga City is a major commercial and industrial hub in Mindanao, with a rapidly growing number of local businesses and entrepreneurs.

Strong Community Spirit

Zamboangueños are known for their resilience, warmth, and bayanihan spirit — qualities that make supporting local businesses a natural part of daily life.

Underserved Digital Market

Despite its size and economic activity, Zamboanga lacked a dedicated local business directory — a gap that DiscoverZamboanga was built to fill.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to list my business?
Yes — completely free. You can submit one business listing at no cost, with no expiration date and no hidden fees. If you need additional listings, you can contact our support team for a manual review.
How long does it take for my listing to go live?
Our team manually reviews every submission to ensure quality. Most listings are reviewed and published within 1–3 business days. You'll be notified once your listing is live.
Can I edit my listing after it's published?
Yes. You can log into your account at any time to update your business information, photos, contact details, and more. Changes may go through a brief review before going live.
What types of businesses can be listed?
Any legitimate business operating in Zamboanga City can be listed — from restaurants, retail shops, and service providers to professional practices, schools, and community organizations.
Who built and manages DiscoverZamboanga?
DiscoverZamboanga was built and is fully managed by RightJobSolutions — a digital solutions company dedicated to building platforms that serve local communities. The platform was developed by a team of interns including Jerico (Zyke) and Incent (Hikusama).
How do I contact support?
You can reach our team through the Contact page. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days.
